You are invited to a Positive Peace Workshop in Queensland, hosted by The Institute of Economics and Peace (IEP) together with Ivory’s Rock Foundation (IRF). It will be held on the weekend of 23rd & 24th August 2019 (dates to be confirmed) at Ivory’s Rock.
This highly engaging workshop will focus on practical efforts to build positive peace in communities, including a session on the importance of personal peace. IEP has collaborated with a number of organisations around the world to deliver similar workshops.
The workshop will be held at Ivory’s Rock Conventions and Events, Peak Crossing. Qld 4306, less than an hour out of Brisbane. This is a unique and world class convention centre which has a conference hall, accomodation and dining.

Overview

The IEP framework for Positive Peace is based on eight pillars which not only sustain peace but also support an environment where human potential can flourish. IEP will introduce these eight positive pillars of peace and explore their application during the workshop
The IRF framework for personal peace focuses on inner resources such as clarity, choice, understanding, courage and hope and how to develop these inner strengths. Participants will be encouraged to share their own personal experiences.

Workshop objectives and outcomes:

The workshop will provide participants with:
1. A thorough understanding of the IEP Pillars of Positive Peace and how to build on the pillars to create and sustain peaceful, cohesive communities.

2. An understanding of the importance of personal peace and recognition of natural attributes such as clarity, gratitude and hope and how to focus on developing these natural attributes (or inner strengths) accessing resources such as PEAK.
